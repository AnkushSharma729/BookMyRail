About Us

BookMyRail is an innovative online platform designed to make railway ticket booking in India easy, fast, and accessible to everyone. Our mission is to provide a seamless digital experience for travelers while keeping convenience and safety at the forefront.

Why Choose BookMyRail?

  • User-Friendly Booking: Book your train tickets quickly through an intuitive web interface.
  • Secure Payments: Pay safely using multiple payment options.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): For users without net banking or card access, COD ensures everyone can book online.
  • Comprehensive Ticket Management: Check PNR status, view bookings, cancel tickets, and get alerts—all in one place.
  • Reliable Customer Support: Our dedicated support team assists you at every step of your journey.
